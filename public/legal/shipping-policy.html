At El Dorado, we're committed to delivering your 963Hz resonant sterling silver jewelry safely and promptly. We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders, with full tracking and insurance included.

2. Processing Time

Your order journey begins the moment you complete checkout:

  • Order Confirmation: Immediate email confirmation upon payment
  • Processing: 1-3 business days to prepare and package your jewelry
  • Quality Check: Each piece is inspected before shipping
  • Packaging: Luxury packaging with protective materials
  • Shipment: Email notification with tracking number once shipped

Rush Orders: Need your jewelry faster? Contact us at tommy@innovlead.ca before ordering for expedited processing options.

6. International Shipping & Customs

6.1 Customs Duties & Taxes

For international orders:

  • Customs Duties: May apply depending on your country's regulations
  • Import Taxes/VAT: Buyer is responsible for any applicable taxes
  • Customs Clearance: Usually takes 1-3 business days
  • Fees Vary: Based on your country's import policies and order value

Important: Customs fees are determined by your country's customs agency, not by El Dorado. We accurately declare all product values to ensure smooth customs processing.

6.2 Customs Documentation

All international packages include:

  • Commercial invoice with accurate product description
  • Declared value in USD
  • HS (Harmonized System) codes for jewelry
  • Country of origin certification

Every shipment is fully insured at no additional cost:

9. Delivery Issues

9.1 Package Not Delivered

If tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your order:

  1. Check with neighbors, building management, or household members
  2. Look for delivery notice or safe-drop location
  3. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered prematurely)
  4. Contact us immediately - we'll investigate and resolve

9.2 Delayed Shipment

If your order is delayed beyond estimated delivery:

  • Check tracking for latest updates
  • Contact us if delayed more than 3 business days past estimate
  • We'll work with carrier to locate and expedite delivery
  • Reship or refund options available if unresolved

9.3 Wrong Address

Provided incorrect shipping address?

  • Before Shipment: Contact us immediately to update (free)
  • After Shipment: Contact carrier to redirect (may incur fees)
  • Cannot be Redirected: Package may return to us; re-shipping fees apply

10. Signature Requirements

For high-value orders ($500+), signature confirmation is required:

  • Someone must be present to sign for delivery
  • Valid ID may be required
  • If missed, carrier will leave delivery notice
  • Package can be picked up at local carrier facility
  • Or schedule re-delivery with carrier

11. PO Boxes & APO/FPO Addresses

PO Boxes

We ship to PO Boxes via postal services:

  • Available for Canada Post and USPS deliveries

APO/FPO Addresses (Military)
