PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: At El Dorado, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you interact with our Website, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Shipping Information: Delivery address, recipient details
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through contact forms, chat, or email
  • Cryptocurrency Transactions: XRP wallet addresses, transaction IDs (if using crypto payment)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit eldorado111.com, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, click patterns, referring website
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies, marketing pixels
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

2.1 Order Processing & Fulfillment

  • Process and complete your purchases
  • Communicate order status and shipping updates
  • Handle returns, exchanges, and refunds
  • Verify payment and prevent fraud

2.2 Customer Service

  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot issues
  • Collect feedback and improve our services

2.3 Marketing & Communications

  • Send promotional emails about new products and special offers (with your consent)
  • Provide personalized product recommendations
  • Conduct market research and analyze customer preferences

2.4 Website Optimization

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Test new features and design elements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Stripe (for credit card processing)
  • Shipping Partners: USPS, FedEx, DHL, or other carriers
  • Email Services: Mailchimp or similar platforms (for newsletters)
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar
  • Hosting Services: Vercel (website hosting)

3.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3.3 Business Transfers

If El Dorado is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

4.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (shopping cart, checkout)
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website usage and performance (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Cookies: Enable targeted advertising (Facebook Pixel, retargeting)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

4.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit website functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using 256-bit SSL
  • Secure Payment Processing: We use Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure (we never store credit card details)
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to customer data
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring for vulnerabilities
  • Cryptocurrency Security: XRP payments are verified on the blockchain

Important: While we take reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Update your account information at any time

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at tommy@innovlead.ca. Note that we may retain certain information for legal compliance.

6.3 Marketing Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in each message
  • Opt out of targeted advertising through your browser settings
  • Contact us to stop all marketing communications

6.4 GDPR Rights (For EU Residents)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes

6.5 CCPA Rights (For California Residents)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Typical Retention Periods:

  • Order data: 7 years (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Marketing data: Until you opt-out or 3 years of inactivity
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)

8. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Privacy Shield certifications (where applicable)
  • Equivalent data protection measures

10. Third-Party Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email or website notice.
