## ðŸ“± Taking Photos (Use Your Phone!)

### Setup:
- **Background:** White wall, black cloth, or marble surface
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight near window (NOT direct sunlight)
- **Time:** 10am-2pm for best light
- **Angle:** Straight on + 45-degree angle

### For Each Product:
1. Clean the jewelry (fingerprints show!)
2. Position on background
3. Take 5-10 shots from different angles
4. Pick the clearest one

### Tips:
- Use portrait mode for blur effect
- Tap to focus on the product
- Keep phone steady (lean on something)
- Take more photos than you need

## ðŸ“ Image Specifications

### Required:
